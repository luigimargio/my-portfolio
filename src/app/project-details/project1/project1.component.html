Treasures of Innocence

In Treasures of Innocence, the player takes on the role of a kid with a passion for archaeology. The game's goal is to dig up, from deep under the sandbox, hidden everyday objects reimagined as ancient artifacts. As more objects are uncovered the player can learn more about the life of the kid hidden behind the innocence of childhood.

Platform: PC

Development Platform: Unity

Development Duration: January 2023 - March 2023

Additional Information

Initial Concept

In this project we had the goal of developing a first-person game with a unique twist: a simulation game with an archaeologist as the main character.

After an intensive brainstorming phase in which we evaluated several ideas, we chose this concept for its feasibility. Being our first real project in the academy, time and resources were limited, so we prioritized a manageable scope.

Gameplay

Treasure Hunting

The player will use maps drawn by the kid to locate the treasures. After having correctly identified the various landmarks, they’ll use their shovel to start digging.

Salvaging and cleaning

Once an artifact has been found the dirt must be carefully cleaned off with the chisel. This is done through a unique puzzle minigame.

Investigation and Storytelling

Found artifacts are catalogued in the archive menu. These items can be further investigated to discover additional information about their history. The overarching narrative and the artifacts’ descriptions work together to open a small window on the kid and their parents’ life with both a light and tragicomic mood.

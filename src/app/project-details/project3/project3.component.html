Darts Town

Embark on a third-person puzzle adventure set on a mysterious archipelago trapped in a time loop. Discover an ancient cursed civilization, interact with intriguing characters, and solve challenging puzzles. Use your wits to stop the loop and save everyone from the apocalypse.

Platform: PC

Development Platform: Unreal Engine

Development Duration: December 2023 - June 2024

My Responsibilities:

  • Worldbuilding
  • Quest
  • Design 3C
  • UI/UX
  • Tuning and Testing

Additional Information

Worldbuilding

In The Last Tide, I contributed to creating a rich narrative universe, where each island represents a core element: body, mind, and soul. This concept shaped the design of the environments, NPCs, and the overarching story. I focused on integrating the lore into the game mechanics, making the world not just a backdrop, but an active participant in the gameplay, where players uncover the mysteries of a cursed archipelago through exploration and interaction.

3C Design

I was responsible for designing the 3C system to ensure smooth and intuitive player movement, camera behavior, and interaction. I designed a third-person camera with dynamic movement, adapting to different tools and gameplay situations, such as boat navigation or puzzle-solving. The control scheme was designed to be accessible while allowing for complex actions like interacting with objects and solving puzzles​.

UI/UX and Journal Design

For the UI/UX, I conducted extensive research on references like Pentiment and Red Dead Redemption II to craft an interface that is both functional and immersive. I designed the journal to provide feedback on player progress, puzzles, and lore, using a handwritten aesthetic to match the game’s tone. My goal was to create a seamless user experience, focusing on clear typography, intuitive feedback, and a minimalist approach to on-screen elements.
